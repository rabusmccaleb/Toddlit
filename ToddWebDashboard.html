Welcome to Toddlit Beta!

Discover a new way to be creative telling story and leverage your skills to shape the minds of the future.

The official release date Toddlit isn’t until December 18th, 2020, So be sure you take your time. Aim to make work that is meaningful and full of deep lessons, that you think are important for the 21st century. We know the world is complicated. We know that everyone has endured through hardship to be here. We want everyone to dig into their hearts and pull out lesson that they never forgot and understandings, they truly hope younger kids never forget either.

On December 18th, We aren’t expecting a big boom, but it should at least give everyone a small understanding of scale and scope of Toddlit as a project.

At Toddlit, we believe in this old idea of “good capitalism”, which only goes to say, good work should be paid for. So as Toddlit scales financially, so do you. We know all the hoops needed to get paid for your work. You can have a Patreon Account. We don’t want you to need one. Initially, the pay will be small, simply because we’ll have a small user base. As Toddlit gains traction, payment will be proportional. So even if you only produce one story and never work with Toddlit again, you’ve made an investment that pays off long term.
